Hackathon September 6th - 8th 2019

JunctionX is a kickoff of Junction, taking place in countries all around the world. Its purpose is to unify and challenge hackers to compete for the common vision of creating something new and groundbreaking. Junction started out in Finland, but over the years the Junction Community has spread worldwide. They wanted to share what they had learned about organizing hackathons, as well as spread the hackathon movement internationally. This is how JunctionX came to be. Since 2018, several teams around the world have created their own hackathons through the JunctionX program: the same Junction mentality, but all events with the personal touch of the local teams.

JunctionX Vienna will bring international developers, designers, artists and scientists together in an environment where they can do things they wouldn't dare to do anywhere else.

It will be an experience that brings the Junction mindset and Viennas quirkiness together to enable a diverse group of people to create bold and unexpected ideas.

A hackathon is a gathering in which small teams (2-5 peers) of developers, designers, entrepreneurs collaboratively code in an extreme manner over a short period of time. It’s a platform for community to come together, learn something new and build a creative and functioning prototype for real world challenges. Don’t expect to have actually solved a problem by the end of the hackathon, real life problems are hard! Think of the hackathon as a training session.
